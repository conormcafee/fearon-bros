Established 1979, Fearon Bros is a family business specialising in the art of creating bespoke kitchen and bedroom furniture.

Our core focus is to tailor make our unique designs to meet the individual needs of our customers; from the timeless traditional, to the ultra modern.

As the kitchen has evolved so to have the materials and appliances that in essence, create it. At Fearon Bros we strive to find new and innovative ways to not only create a kitchen, but a living space.

Our Approach

Our dedication to pushing the boundairies of design is the core focus of our approach. During the design consultation phase we discuss your lifestyle, your design ideas, and what you want from your kitchen.

We take the time to get a better understanding of the way you live so we can tailor your design to work in harmony with your home.
